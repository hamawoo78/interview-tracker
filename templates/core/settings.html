About Timezone Detection
How it works:
  • When you log in, your IP address is used to determine your geographic location
  • Your timezone is automatically set based on your location
  • All interview dates and times are displayed in your local timezone
  • You can disable auto-detection and manually select a timezone
Privacy:
  • IP geolocation uses free public APIs (ip-api.com, ipapi.co)
  • Only your IP address is sent to these services
  • Results are cached for 24 hours to reduce API calls
  • No personal data is stored or shared
Supported Timezones:

All IANA timezone identifiers are supported (e.g., America/New_York, Europe/London, Asia/Tokyo, etc.)
